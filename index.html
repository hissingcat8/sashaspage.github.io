Sasha

One sunny day in December of 2012 I found you. You were walking outside my apartment building, rummaging through piles of garbage looking for something to eat. I remember going upstairs to my apartment and calling my then girlfriend and going

  • "There's this cute little puppy outside my building. I really want to bring her home with me. What do you think?"

And that's how this journey started. You were going to be here for a few days while we found you a forever home. A few days turned into weeks, then months, and now it's been almost 13 years. The cute puppy is now a senior dog. Her face has turned gray and her joins are starting to give out a little. Despite all this, her excitement when I grab the leash to take for a walk is still the same: jumping up and down, howling and barking.

I'll cut this story short because I don't know what else to write. The end.
